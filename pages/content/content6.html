06 Redesign

This is a redesign of a website I made in collab with two classmates. The client was a clinic consisting of chiropractors, physiotherapists, masseuses etc.

The design needed to be simple and with calm colours, to match the target group.

I did most of the wireframing and mockups because of a scarce time limit. In the end, I think it turned out pretty well.
