02 Graphical Poster

I love contrast, colour, shapes and bold typography. In this design, I tried combining all four. I've always found a lot of inspiration in graphical posters. I love how simple shapes can tell a story, which can be interpretated differently, depending on the viewer.

This design is very simple, yet it awakens a feeling of happiness in me. What a cliché thing to say. But it's the truth.

I think it's important we as humans make beautiful stuff to look at, and I don't necessarily believe it has to have a deeper meaning or a specific purpose.

Thanks for stopping by!
